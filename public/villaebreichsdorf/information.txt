Our Story
A Place Worth Fighting For
An elegant villa in Ebreichsdorf, Austria—just 20 minutes from Vienna—now caught in bankruptcy proceedings. Together, we can give it a new future.

Villa Ebreichsdorf - Seepromenade 109
Location: Seepromenade 109, 2483 Ebreichsdorf, Austria • Plot: 1,215 m² • Living Space: 346 m² + 200 m² terraces

The Situation
This neoclassical villa with its elegant columns, solar-equipped roof, and panoramic terraces was built as a dream home in a private lakeside community. When the owner company (Royal smart House GmbH) entered bankruptcy in September 2021, the property fell into uncertainty.

The exterior is fully complete—with pool, photovoltaic system, and professional landscaping. The interior awaits finishing. With a court-certified appraisal of €1,384,300 (January 2025), this represents a unique opportunity.

Why We Stepped In
We believe this villa deserves more than a conventional sale. That's why we're creating a foundation—to give everyone the chance to be part of something special: a community-owned sustainable retreat just outside Vienna.
