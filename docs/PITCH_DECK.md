Convert to PDF locally when needed: `npx markdown-pdf docs/PITCH_DECK.md -o docs/PITCH_DECK.pdf`

---

## Slide 1 — Problem

- Institutional real-estate yields are gated; retail lacks transparent, compliant access to verifiable RWAs on-chain.
- DAOs need **trust-minimized vaults**, **strategy allocation**, and **transparent reserves** — not opaque spreadsheets.

---

## Slide 2 — Solution / Product

- **Onchain Savings Club** on Base: USDC vault (`SavingsVault`), DAO governance, strategy registry, and optional **Villa POC** bonding curve for fractional exposure tied to a real-asset narrative.
- Member-facing dashboard: live TVL, protocol pulse, reserves, Academy, and on-chain verification links.

---

## Slide 3 — Traction & On-Chain

- Contracts deployed on Base (chain id 8453): vault, treasury, governance, strategy registry, learning NFTs; optional villa POC curve when configured.
- Live metrics via **GET /api/protocol/pulse** and portfolio reads — TVL and curve price visible in-app (`VaultWidget`).
